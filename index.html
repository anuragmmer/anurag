anuragmmer/bpm-analyser

Designed offers an analysis of BPM (Beats Per Minute) by examining the segments of an audio file. The script provides detailed insights, including overall BPM, modal BPM, standard deviation, and much more.

anuragmmer/timed-lyrics

Designed for creators, editors, and developers who need to transcribe audio files with precise timings and integrate them into Adobe After Effects with automation or anywhere else manually.

anuragmmer/pomodoro

A simple pomodoro timer for studying, reading and focusing.

anuragmmer/click-counter

A simple and interactive web page that allows users to create a dynamic grid of buttons with individual click counters and the functionality to save the data in a spreadsheet.

Thinking in Systems: A Primer

[1] • [BOOK]

Meadows did an amazing job, providing a lifetime's worth of knowledge that makes sense of the complexity of the world by looking at it as systems and discovering the relationships between its different parts.
The Cult of Done: How To Get *Started*

[2] • [YOUTUBE]]

Join "The Cult of Done." The Cult of Done Manifesto is a special document written by Bre Pettis and Kio Stark in 2009. It outlines 13 principles for getting creative things done. The manifesto has helped me deeply understand and execute the act of creating.
The Almanac of Naval Ravikant

[3] • [BOOK]

A life-changing philosophical book by Eric Jorgenson, this book collects and curates Naval’s wisdom from Twitter, Podcasts, and Essays over the past decade. It changed everything for me, single-handedly shaped my inner belief system and continues to influence me even after four years.
